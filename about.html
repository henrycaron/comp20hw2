Welcome to Hank's Tow Truck Company!

Hank and Marky in 1992

Our company was started by Hanky and Marky in December of 1992.

Since then, we have been towing all over the Boston Area, from Saugus to South End.

We pride ourselves on having the BIGGEST trucks that can tow the HEAVIEST vehicles.

Our proudest achievement was towing an 18-wheeler from Foxborough to our base in Medford.

Today, we have over 50 tow trucks operating at all times. We tow FAST, WELL, and love seeing people's faces when they find out their vehicle has been towed.
